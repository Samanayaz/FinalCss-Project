Basic Design Principles

When you know the basic interior design principles you can transform any space to look fabulous. You know what it feels like when you walk into a well-designed room. You can sense how everything feels cohesive and put together.

Balance

In design, a balance creates a feeeling of equilibrium. It is all about equalizing or approximating the visual weight of objects.
